My Experience

Communication Equals Collaboration

I’ve found effective communication to be just about the most important skill in my life – personal and professional. Extensive study and varied experience provide the tools of the craft. My work ethic, creativity, judgment and unique perspective ultimately deliver the goods.

Having trained as a journalist, my approach to any project is straightforward and well-researched. I am neither afraid to ask questions of others nor to answer for my own decisions. Past positions have tested my adaptability. Whether discussing a project one-on-one over coffee or making story pitches to large groups; hosting client updates in the conference room or having a team meeting over Skype; presenting to small children or chaperoning young adults; I’m up for it all.

To be communicative is to be collaborative. I perform best in a team and value work environment right up there with the work itself. My interests are broad, and my experience, while all rooted in communication, spans the written, the verbal and the visual.

Contact me if you would like to work together.
